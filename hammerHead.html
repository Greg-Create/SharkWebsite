Hammer Head Shark

Price: $23,300

The Hammerhead Shark is one of the most distinctive and recognizable species of shark in the ocean. With its flat, hammer-shaped head, this shark is known for its unique appearance and powerful swimming abilities. Hammerheads are found in warm waters around the world, and are known to be excellent hunters. Their broad, flat heads contain a network of sensors that allow them to detect the electrical fields generated by their prey, such as stingrays and other fish. Once they've located their target, Hammerheads use their powerful jaws to quickly capture and consume their prey.

Despite their fearsome reputation, Hammerhead Sharks can make great pets for experienced aquarium owners. They are hardy and adaptable, and can thrive in a properly maintained aquarium. However, due to their size and swimming requirements, they require a large tank with plenty of open space to swim and explore.
