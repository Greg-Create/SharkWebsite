Tiger Shark

Price: $17,800

The Tiger Shark is a powerful and majestic species of shark, named for its striking tiger-like stripes on its body. Found in warm waters around the world, the Tiger Shark is known for its voracious appetite and its ability to consume a wide variety of prey, from fish and crustaceans to sea turtles and even dolphins. Tiger Sharks are known for their excellent swimming abilities, and are able to cover great distances in search of food. Their sharp, serrated teeth allow them to tear through tough prey with ease, and their powerful jaws can exert an incredible amount of force.

While Tiger Sharks are not recommended as pets for most aquarium owners, they can be kept in large, properly maintained tanks by experienced enthusiasts. These sharks require a large amount of swimming space, as well as a varied diet and careful monitoring to ensure their health and well-being. At our website, we offer a variety of Tiger Sharks for sale, including juvenile and adult specimens. We work closely with certified breeders and importers to ensure that all of our sharks are ethically sourced and healthy.
